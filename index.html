Our Controlled Manufacturing Process

From lab to field, every batch ensures purity and maximum biological activity.

1

Microbial Isolation

Pure, high-potency strains are isolated and checked for genetic stability and bioactivity in our certified lab.

2

Large-Scale Fermentation

Bacteria are cultured in sterile, state-of-the-art fermenters under strictly monitored temperature and nutrient conditions.

3

Carrier Formulation

The concentrated culture is mixed with bio-degradable carriers (liquid or powder) to ensure maximum shelf life and field efficacy.

4

Quality Assurance & Dispatch

Final product quality is verified (viability, purity, count) before sealed packaging and dispatch to the distribution network.
